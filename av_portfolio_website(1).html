About Me

I'm an Audio Visual Technician and music producer with a strong background in live event support, technical troubleshooting, and creative media production. My experience spans operating sound systems, projection and display setups, video switchers, and lighting rigs for events of all sizes, as well as managing AV installations and ensuring flawless technical execution under tight deadlines.

With a foundation in IT, I bring a strong understanding of networking, hardware configuration, and system integration to my AV work—whether it's running live audio, setting up multi-camera streaming, or maintaining complex AV control systems. I've also produced and released original music to an audience of over 200,000 followers, giving me a unique creative edge in sound design, editing, and media presentation.

I take pride in combining technical skill with adaptability and clear communication, ensuring every project—whether for a client, audience, or production team—runs seamlessly from setup to final performance.

Skills & Services

I provide complete audio visual solutions for live events, corporate functions, and creative projects. My skills include live sound mixing, digital and analog console operation, microphone setup, and audio editing using tools like Ableton Live, FL Studio, and Adobe Audition. I specialize in projector and LED wall setups, video switching, multi-camera streaming, and stage lighting design with DMX programming. With a strong IT background, I also handle AV networking, hardware installation, system integration, and troubleshooting for both live and installed systems. In addition to technical support, I offer creative services including custom audio production, music creation, and content development to bring events and media projects to life.
